Welcome to Christ Boys..

Christ’s school boys’ section is governed by a well known man of God in the Anglican Diocese of Ekiti whose name is VENERABLE OLUROTIMI OSO EBENEZER. The principal of the school who is an Alumnus of the school, has been the best the school has ever received as a leader of value and moral knowledge. He has been able to restore the Glory of the school back to its original position. Christ’s School boys’ section yearly produces at least over 300 graduating students. The school has recorded a 98% success in the last UNIFIED Examination, and also had over 500 students who passed the Junior WAEC Examination excellently.
